# Private Blockchain

## Competences
- Hyperledger Fabric (permissioned network)
- Hyperledger Besu (Enterprise Ethereum)
- Substrate (custom chain)
- Consensus mechanisms (PBFT, Raft, PoA)
- Smart contracts (Solidity, Chaincode Go)
- Signature cryptographique des transactions
- Verification d'integrite de chaine (hash chain validation)
- Channel management et privacy

## Verification cryptographique
- Validation des blocs (hash integrity)
- Verification des signatures de transactions
- Merkle tree proofs
- Chain consistency checks
- Double-spend detection
- Fork detection et resolution

## Use Cases
- Supply chain traceability
- Document notarization
- Identity management (DID, Verifiable Credentials)
- Internal token economy
- Audit trail immutable
- Signature cryptographique des transactions agents
