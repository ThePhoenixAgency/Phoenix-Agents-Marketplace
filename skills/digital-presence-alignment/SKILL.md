## Semantic Alignment (AIO)
The goal is to ensure content is perfectly digestible for Large Language Models (LLM) and specialized crawlers:
1. **JSON-LD & Schema**: Systematic integration of structured schemas to facilitate context comprehension by agents.
2. **AI Crawl Policy**: Precise `robots.txt` configuration to guide agents like `GPTBot`, `ClaudeBot`, etc.
3. **Deep Sitemaps**: Generation of segmented sitemaps for exhaustive discovery of business logic.

## SEO Scrutiny Protocol
- **Metadata Audit**: Continuous verification of aligned OpenGraph and Twitter Card tags.
- **Semantic Transparency**: Use of clear, unambiguous language to prevent indexing hallucinations.
- **Sitemap Scrutiny**: Scripted validation of link integrity and index segment refresh.

## Induction Tools
- `node scripts/seo-scrutiny.js`: Analyzes repository compliance with digital presence standards.
- `robots.txt`: Minimalist, pure quality policy for AI bots.
