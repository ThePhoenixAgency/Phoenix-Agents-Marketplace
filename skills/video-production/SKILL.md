# Video Production

## Competences
- Script writing (YouTube, TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Video editing automation (FFmpeg, MoviePy)
- Sous-titrage automatique (Whisper transcription)
- Thumbnail generation
- Text-to-video (Runway, Pika, Kling)
- Screen recording et tutoriels
- Video repurposing (long -> shorts, clips)
- Audio mixing et background music
- Intro/outro generation

## Formats
- YouTube (16:9, 1080p/4K)
- TikTok/Reels/Shorts (9:16, 1080x1920)
- Stories (9:16, 15s/30s/60s)
- Cours en ligne (screen recording + face cam)
